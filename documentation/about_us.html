OUR TEAM: United by Knowledge, Powered by Diversity

We are a dedicated group of engineering students from ENSA Marrakech, Morocco, specializing in Embedded Systems and Control Engineering. Distributed across three core domains — electronics, IT, and mechanics — we combine our strengths to tackle complex challenges with creativity, precision, and teamwork.
This is more than a project — it's a collective vision driven by cultural richness and technical excellence. As a ten-member team, we are participating in an African inter-university competition that brings together students from across the continent. This experience allows us to put our skills into practice while fostering mutual learning and continental exchange.
